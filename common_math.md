## Inline Math

## Variables
* **x bar** x̄: `\bar{x}`
* **x hat** x̂: `\hat{x}`
* **x tilde** x̃: `\tilde{x}`
* **x dot** ẋ: `\dot{x}`
* **Vector x** **x**: `\mathbf{x}`
* **Partial** ∂: `\partial`
* **Infinity** ∞: `\infty`

## Operators & Relations
* **Less than equals** ≤: `\leq`
* **Greater than equals** ≥: `\geq`
* **Not equal** ≠: `\neq`
* **Approximately** ≈: `\approx`
* **Sum (inline)** ∑: `\sum`
* **Product (inline)** ∏: `\prod`
* **Right arrow** →: `\to`
* **Maps to** ↦: `\mapsto`
* **Implies** ⇒: `\implies`
* **If and only if** ⇔: `\iff`

## Greek Letters (Common)
Alpha α: \alpha
Beta β: \beta
Delta (change) Δ: \Delta / δ: \delta
Epsilon (error) ε: \epsilon
Theta (angle/Big-O) θ: \theta
Lambda (eigenvalue) λ: \lambda
Mu (mean) μ: \mu
Pi π: \pi
Rho (correlation) ρ: \rho
Sigma σ: \sigma
Tau τ: \tau
Phi ϕ: \phi
Omega (complexity) Ω: \Omega / ω: \omega

## Set Theory
* **Subset** ⊂: `\subset`
* **Subset equals** ⊆: `\subseteq`
* **Element of** ∈: `\in`
* **Not an element of** ∉: `\notin`
* **Empty set** ∅: `\emptyset`
* **Intersection** ∩: `\cap`
* **Union** ∪: `\cup`
* **Real numbers** ℝ: `\mathbb{R}`
* **Integers** ℤ: `\mathbb{Z}`
* **Natural numbers** ℕ: `\mathbb{N}`

## Logic & Discrete Math
* **For all** ∀: `\forall`
* **There exists** ∃: `\exists`
* **Logical NOT** ¬: `\neg`
* **Logical AND** ∧: `\land`
* **Logical OR** ∨: `\lor`
* **Ceiling** ⌈ ⌉: `\lceil \rceil`
* **Floor** ⌊ ⌋: `\lfloor \rfloor`

## Display Style Operators
> [!NOTE] 
> These are best used in double dollar signs `$$...$$` for centered, large formatting.

* **Summation** ∑: `\displaystyle \sum_{i=0}^{n}`
* **Product** ∏: `\displaystyle \prod_{i=0}^{n}`
* **Integral** ∫: `\displaystyle \int_{a}^{b}`
* **Limit** lim: `\displaystyle \lim_{n \to \infty}`
